Nestled in the brand new Weston Common development – winner of the 2017 BILD Award for Best New Planned Community – is West22. A resort-style, purpose-built rental building that has everything you want, right on site. At 30 storeys high, with sweeping views of the city and thoughtful suite designs, West22 offers a lifestyle of convenience beyond compare.
