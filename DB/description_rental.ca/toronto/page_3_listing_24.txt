Valencia Towers is a student- and family-friendly apartment community featuring cozy suites, coveted on-site amenities, and an excellent Bathurst Street location.
Choose from bachelor, 1, 2 and 3 bedroom suites to suit your and your family's needs. These pet friendly and renovated apartment homes feature spacious layouts and private balconies. Condo-style on-site amenities include a gym, laundry facilities, and an on-site daycare, all under professional on-site management. 
This Certified Rental Building is located near Bathurst and Steeles in bustling North York. Students and staff can reach York University in 20 minutes by transit or 10 minutes by car. Valencia Towers is also close to shopping, grocery, restaurants, banks, pharmacies, and entertainment, including the G Ross Lord Park nearby.
